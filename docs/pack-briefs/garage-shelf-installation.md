## Positioning

**One drill, one afternoon, a clean garage.** A small-equipment business: a few
hundred dollars of tools lets you deliver a highly visible transformation people
gladly pay for and neighbours instantly want. The before/after sells the next job.

## The offer & deliverable

Mount manufactured shelving, overhead racks, and hooks to get everything off the
floor. Deliverable = a tidy, level, organised storage wall + before/after photos.
Fixed-scope tiers (single-wall → full storage wall → per-property bundle).

## Pricing (as built into the Quote Calculator)

- Single-bay install ~$350 labour · Double ~$550 · Premium storage wall ~$850.
- Labour priced separately from materials (materials at cost + ~15% handling).
- Add-ons: overhead rack ~$150, pegboard ~$120, hooks ~$60, workbench ~$180.
- Reel earnings framing: ~$500/install, ~8/week → ~$4,000/week, ~$16,000/month, ~$192,000/year; scale by hiring + training another installer.

## ⚠️ Claim-safety & scope (critical)

Baked into the FAQs, Install & Safety Checklist, and AI Prompt Pack:

- **Manufactured-product install only** — not structural, load-bearing, or custom carpentry. Refer those to a licensed builder/engineer.
- **Locate services before drilling** — scan for electrical cables and water/gas pipes; assume cable runs from outlets/switches/board; check the opposite wall side.
- **Anchor to wall type** — studs where possible; rated toggles for plasterboard; masonry anchors for brick/block. Never exceed product/fixing weight ratings.
- **Heavy/overhead loads** — ceiling-mounted racks and heavy loads may need a qualified trade; check local building rules for permanent/overhead installs.
- Client confirms wall suitability and contents; not responsible for pre-existing defects.
